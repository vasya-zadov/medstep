Services

Medical Care

Placebo Nursing Home offers 24 hours dedicated medical care for all residents. Special cares can be provided as well.

Daily Assistance

Assistance with all activities of daily living, which include bathing, dressing, toileting, hygiene and transferring.

Activities

Activities, including daily exercise, watching movies, listening to music, reminiscing and non-denominational services.

Events

Crafts courses, computer and language lessons, balls, Social Soiree Nights, Opera and theater visits and many more.

Placebo Nursing Home Houses

Why to choose us

Placebo Nursing Home offers 1 floor houses with 2 rooms and additional kitchen. Placebo Nursing Home provides 24 hours professional medical care for our residents.


Why we are better

Among all other great features we provide room service, housekeeping, television and internet access, telephone, walk-in closet, hairdresser, spa and recreational activities.

  • 2 standard rooms - bedroom, living room + kitchen
  • Balcony and free parking - mountain view
  • Housekeeping - daily
  • Television + Internet + Telephone - satelite and viber
  • 24 hours medical services - best specialists

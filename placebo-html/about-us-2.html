Placebo Clinic is known for it's responsibility, devotion and will to make things better. Equipped with the latest technologies and the best professionals in every medical field, Placebo is the most competitive hospital in the country. With almost 240 staff and management members Placebo is striving to provide the best care a patient can get. We are working closely with foreign specialists to constantly exchange experience and develop new practices and procedures. The structure of the hospital is multidisciplinary and almost all medical disciplines are covered here.


I have a dream that my four little children will one day live in a nation where they will not be judged by the color of their skin, but by the content of their character.

Phillipe Madison Placebo Managing Director
